Courses

Chinese Mandarin

Learn how to speak and understand Mandarin Chinese from someone who has studied the language for over 10 years. Beginner and intermediate students are welcome to take this course to further their understanding of the language. Reading and writing is optional.

Programming

Students in this course will learn the basic fundamentals of programming, how to write clean, readable, and reusable code, as well as more advance material for students at that level, such as Object Oriented Programming (OOP).

Math

From fractions and simple arithmetic to trigonometry, make math fun again and easy to understand in this course. Learn how to do mental math with someone who knows and loves math and is passionate about helping others understand.
